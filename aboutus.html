ABOUT US

who are we?

Here at cards gallery, we are a unique community of artists, writers, musicians, marketing and technology professionals, dedicated to providing you with fun and imaginative card designs and an engaging site experience.

what we believe

We believe that wishes are granted, dreams come true and anything is possible. We know that not every day is a holiday, but every day is a good day and so we celebrate, comfort and commemorate.

Why We're Here

We are passionate about what we do. We work hard every day to make the world a more thoughtful and caring place. Together with our members, we share the conviction that nurturing relationships and making a difference in people’s lives, is the most important way we can spend our time.

Our Promise to You

We are committed to designing each card with you in mind, drawing on our many years of experience and the close connection we feel with our Blue Mountain friends. Because online greetings have the power to reach your loved ones wherever they may be, whenever they need it most, we promise to be there for you with authentic and artistic ways to keep you close and connected to all the special people in your life.
